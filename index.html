📚 Laptop Buying Guide 2026

🎯 How to Choose the Right Laptop (My Process)

Step 1: Define Your Budget

  • Under ₹45K: Dell Inspiron, HP 15s (basic tasks)
  • ₹45-70K: HP Victus, ASUS VivoBook (gaming, creative)
  • Above ₹1L: MacBook Air, Dell XPS (premium, professional)

Step 2: Know Your Use Case

  • Student/Office: i3/Ryzen 3 + 8GB RAM + 256GB SSD (₹35-45K)
  • Gaming: Dedicated GPU (GTX 1650+) + 16GB RAM (₹65K+)
  • Video Editing: i5/Ryzen 5+ + 16GB RAM + Dedicated GPU (₹70K+)
  • Programming: i5/Ryzen 5 + 16GB RAM + 512GB SSD (₹50-60K)

Step 3: Check These BEFORE Buying

  • ✅ SSD, not HDD: SSDs are 10x faster (non-negotiable in 2026)
  • ✅ Battery reviews: Ignore manufacturer claims, check Amazon reviews
  • ✅ Build quality: Plastic flexes, aluminum doesn't
  • ✅ Warranty: Min 1-year onsite (Dell, HP offer this)
  • ✅ Return policy: Amazon offers 10-day returns (test thoroughly!)
