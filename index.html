❓ FAQ: Common Questions About Laptops

Should I choose Windows, macOS, or Linux for 2026?

Windows: Best for gaming, software compatibility, and affordability. Works with most peripherals and software. Recommended for most users.

macOS: Best for creative professionals (video editing, design). Excellent ecosystem integration. Premium pricing.

Linux: Best for developers and tech enthusiasts. Free and highly customizable. Smaller software library.

How much RAM do I really need?

8GB: Sufficient for web browsing, office work, and streaming. Budget option.

16GB: Recommended for multitasking, coding, and content creation. Future-proof.

32GB+: For professionals: video editors, software developers, data scientists.

SSD vs HDD: What's the difference?

SSD (Solid State Drive): Faster boot times (10-20 seconds), quick app loading (2-3 seconds). Modern standard. More expensive per GB.

HDD (Hard Disk Drive): Slower (30-60 second boot), mechanical parts can fail. Cheaper. Obsolete for new laptops.

Recommendation: Always choose SSD for new laptops.

Are refurbished laptops worth buying?

Refurbished laptops can offer good value if they come with warranty (1-2 years) and from trusted sellers. Check Amazon Renewed for verified products. Expect 20-30% discount over new.
