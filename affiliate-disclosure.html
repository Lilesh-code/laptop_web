💰 How We Make Money

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking links that allow us to earn a commission when you purchase products through them.

Here's Exactly How It Works:

  1. You click our "Check Latest Price" or "Shop Now" button
  2. You're redirected to Amazon.in (same product, same price)
  3. You make a purchase (within 24 hours of clicking)
  4. Amazon pays us a small commission (3-4% of sale price)
  5. You pay nothing extra - the price is identical to direct Amazon links

📊 Real Example:

Let's say you buy a laptop for ₹50,000 through our link:

  • You pay Amazon: ₹50,000 (same as non-affiliate price)
  • Amazon pays us: ₹1,500-2,000 (3-4% commission)
  • Your cost: ₹50,000 (NO markup, NO hidden fees)

Result: You get same product at same price. We earn commission that funds our research and keeps this guide free.
