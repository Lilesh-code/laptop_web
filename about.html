🔬 My Testing Process

Here's how I actually test laptops (not just read specs):

1️⃣ Real-World Usage

  • Daily tasks for 2-4 weeks
  • Battery drain tests (screen at 50%)
  • Heat monitoring under load
  • Keyboard comfort (typing tests)

2️⃣ Performance Testing

  • CPU benchmarks (Cinebench, Geekbench)
  • Gaming tests (if GPU available)
  • Video editing (Premiere, DaVinci)
  • Programming (VS Code, Android Studio)

3️⃣ Build Quality Check

  • Materials (plastic vs aluminum)
  • Hinge durability
  • Flex test (lid, keyboard deck)
  • Port placement & quality

4️⃣ Amazon Reviews Analysis

  • Read 500+ buyer reviews
  • Identify common problems
  • Verify manufacturer claims
  • Check long-term reliability

What I DON'T Do:

  • ❌ Copy-paste manufacturer marketing claims
  • ❌ Recommend laptops I haven't researched thoroughly
  • ❌ Hide cons to boost affiliate sales
  • ❌ Recommend expensive = better (often budget wins)
